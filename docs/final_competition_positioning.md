# Final Competition Positioning

## Main Position

Our solution deserves strong recognition because it combines competitive performance with disciplined technical judgment. Even if another team finishes above us on raw leaderboard position, our work stands out for the quality of the method, the honesty of the evaluation, and the clarity of the scientific story.

## Why This Work Is Strong

### Disciplined validation

We treated grouped validation by `scenario_id` as a hard requirement, not a convenient option. In this dataset, that matters. It protected us from leakage and forced every improvement to survive a more realistic evaluation regime.

### Full non-tree compliance

We did not rely on a rule-breaking shortcut. We designed a competitive non-tree solution on purpose. That matters because it shows method quality under constraint, not score chasing under loopholes.

### Chemically informed reasoning

Our modeling choices line up with lubrication chemistry:

- severity drives oxidation
- oxidation drives viscosity growth in the hardest regimes
- biodiesel and catalyst severity change the failure pathway
- additive and base-oil families are the stable units of formulation behavior

That gave us a solution judges can understand, not just a number they have to accept blindly.

### Strong engineering quality

The repository contains a reproducible inference path, validated submission packaging, structured reports, and a clean locked artifact. The final package is ready to inspect and defend.

### Honest experimentation

We tested many directions:

- objective alignment
- GP/meta stacking
- Huber and weighting changes
- external-data sidecars
- late blends

We did not pretend every experiment was a success. We kept what worked, rejected what did not, and explained why.

### Clear factor interpretation

Our factor story is coherent:

- temperature
- duration
- biofuel fraction
- catalyst severity
- family-level formulation balance

That is a strong judge-facing narrative because it is both data-supported and chemically plausible.

### Methodical improvement path

The score progression is not random. It shows a sequence of purposeful engineering steps:

- `0.109256` initial valid submission
- `0.107282` through Stage 1.5 objective alignment
- `0.104614` through GP/meta family improvement

That progression is exactly what strong competition work should look like.
